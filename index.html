三角関数

正弦・余弦

\begin{align*} \sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta&=1 \label{1}\\ \sin(-\theta)&=-\sin\theta \\ \cos(-\theta)&=\cos\theta \\ \sin(\theta+\frac{\pi}{2})&=\cos\theta \\ \cos(\theta+\frac{\pi}{2})&=-\sin\theta \\ \sin(\theta+\pi)&=-\sin\theta \\ \cos(\theta+\pi)&=-\cos\theta \\ \end{align*}
